# MedCare+ User Guide

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the MedCare+ website
2. Click "Register" in the top right corner
3. Fill in your details:
   - Email address
   - Password (must be at least 6 characters)
4. Click "Create Account"

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" in the top right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Subscription Plans

### Free Plan Features

The free plan includes basic medication management:
- Add and manage medications
- Basic adherence tracking
- Simple reminders
- Basic analytics
- Standard notifications

### Premium Plan Features

Upgrade to premium for advanced features:
- **Advanced Analytics**: Detailed insights and trend analysis
- **Data Export**: Export your medication and adherence data as PDF
- **Custom Reminders**: Personalized reminder schedules and settings
- **Risk Predictions**: AI-powered adherence risk assessment
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times for support requests

### Upgrading to Premium

1. Go to the Subscription page
2. Choose your preferred payment method:
   - **Stripe** (Credit/Debit Cards)
   - **MercadoPago** (Latin America)
3. Select your currency (USD, EUR, BRL, ARS, etc.)
4. Click "Upgrade to Premium"
5. Complete the payment process
6. Your account will be upgraded immediately

### Payment Methods

#### Stripe (Global)
- Accepts major credit and debit cards
- Secure payment processing
- Available worldwide
- Automatic monthly billing

#### MercadoPago (Latin America)
- Popular in Latin American countries
- Multiple payment options
- Local currency support
- Secure and reliable

### Managing Your Subscription

1. Go to your Profile page
2. Click "Subscription Management"
3. View your current plan and billing information
4. Cancel or modify your subscription as needed

## Managing Medications

### Adding a Medication

1. Click "Add Medication" on the dashboard
2. Fill in the medication details:
   - Medication name
   - Dosage
   - Frequency
   - Start date
   - End date (optional)
   - Notes (optional)
3. Click "Save"

### Editing a Medication

1. Go to the Medications page
2. Find the medication you want to edit
3. Click the edit (pencil) icon
4. Update the details
5. Click "Save"

### Deleting a Medication

1. Go to the Medications page
2. Find the medication you want to delete
3. Click the delete (trash) icon
4. Confirm deletion

## Tracking Adherence

### Logging Medication Intake

1. Go to the Dashboard
2. Find the medication in the "Today's Medications" section
3. Click "Taken" when you take the medication
4. Add any notes if needed

### Viewing Adherence History

1. Go to the Adherence page
2. View your adherence history:
   - Daily view
   - Weekly view
   - Monthly view
3. Filter by medication if needed

### Adherence Statistics

- **Overall Adherence Rate**: Percentage of medications taken on time
- **Medication-Specific Rates**: Individual medication adherence
- **Time-based Patterns**: When you typically take medications
- **Missed Doses Tracking**: Detailed missed dose history

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Can't Log In**
   - Check your email and password
   - Make sure your email is verified
   - Try resetting your password
   - Clear browser cache

2. **Notifications Not Working**
   - Check notification settings
   - Ensure browser permissions
   - Verify email settings
   - Check device notification settings

3. **Data Not Syncing**
   - Check internet connection
   - Refresh the page
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try logging out and back in

4. **Payment Issues**
   - Verify card information
   - Check with your bank for any blocks
   - Try a different payment method
   - Contact support if problems persist

5. **Premium Features Not Available**
   - Verify your subscription status
   - Check payment confirmation
   - Refresh the page
   - Contact support if issues persist
